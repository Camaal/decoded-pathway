"A Political Scientists is someone who Study the origin, development, and operation of political systems. May study topics, such as public opinion, political decisionmaking, and ideology. May analyze the structure and operation of governments, as well as various political entities. May conduct public opinion surveys, analyze election results, or analyze public documents. . The Political Scientists  occupation fits a more significant career segment of Life, Physical, and Social Science Occupations. This occupation is considered part of job zone 5, described as occupations that need extensive preparation.
