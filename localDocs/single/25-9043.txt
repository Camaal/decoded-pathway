"A Teaching Assistants, Special Education is someone who Assist a preschool, elementary, middle, or secondary school teacher to provide academic, social, or life skills to students who have learning, emotional, or physical disabilities. Serve in a position for which a teacher has primary responsibility for the design and implementation of educational programs and services. . The Teaching Assistants, Special Education  occupation fits a more significant career segment of Educational Instruction and Library Occupations.
